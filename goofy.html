Goofy Boarding

Ecommerce Skate Shop

Goofy Boarding Co. is a skate brand I launched in 2019. Primarily selling goods online, my goal was to build a website that gives customers a simple yet unique shopping experience. I followed more traditional UX design standards in areas like page layout and color scheme, and added my own flare with font type, widgets, and more.
