SK8 RATZ

NFT Minting Dapp & more

SK8 RATZ is a collection of 3000 non-fungible tokens (NFTs) I created in 2022. To bring the idea to life, I designed over 200 character traits, branding assets, UI/UX and more. Users can seamlessly connect to the smart contract and purchase the NFTs with their web3 wallet, as well as browse the other pages and learn more about SK8 RATZ. My toolkit for this project included React.js, Node.js, Ethers.js, HTML, Javascript, CSS, Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop, Solidity, IPFS, and Python.
