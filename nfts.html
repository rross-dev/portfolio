SK8 RATZ

Generative NFT Art

SK8 RATZ is a collection of 3000 non-fungible tokens (NFTs) I created in 2022. For the artwork, I designed over 200 character traits (body type, eyes, mouth, board, etc.) in Adobe Illustrator, and wrote a Python script that outputs the final generative artworks based on predetermined trait rarities I set. The result is 3000 completely unique SK8 RATZ characters.
