Gwei.boo

Ethereum Gas Price App

This progressive web app was born out of personal desire for a quick and clean way to check the current Ethereum gas price within seconds. Created with HTML, Javascript, and CSS - Gwei.boo effortlessly connects to the Etherscan API, refreshing with the most up to date Ethereum gas prices every 5 seconds.
